About Me

Hi my name is Benny, I was born and live in Yogyakarta. I am a graduate of Bachelor of Informatics at Mercubuana University, Yogyakarta. I have an interest in Information and Technology. I have skills in various IT fields including networking, web/application programming and UI/UX design.

I have had various experiences, one of which is being a Fiber Technician at an internet service provider company. I learned from designing and then building a good network according to established standards to collecting data and doing maintenance and repair of network devices. I am very familiar with routers, UTP and Fiber Cables.

I also love to design and build web applications to help simplify and speed up and maintain the accuracy of the data you need in your business according to what you need. The application that I created is a new and effective breakthrough that has been needed so far. Let's communicate further to discuss and launch our big project.
